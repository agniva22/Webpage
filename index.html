About Me

I am a Ph.D. scholar specializing in robotics and automation at IISER Bhopal. My research focuses on multi-agent reinforcement learning, particularly applied to the problem of generalized persistent monitoring within autonomous vehicle systems. This work has significant implications for enhancing the capabilities of cooperative autonomous vehicle fleets, with potential applications in areas such as traffic management, surveillance, and smart transportation networks.
